CiTY is an eclectic amalgamation of my experiences and imagination. I aimed to design an engaging landscape that has an overarching goal for the user along with other activities and sightseeing opportunities nested inside. Ultimately, the stories and visuals that I produced for CiTY can only be experienced in Virtual Reality. CiTY evokes a sense of familiarity and unfamiliarity, as my goal is to craft a story that resonates with the user and reverberates in the user’s mind.


Transformations and scripts are crucial to the virtual environment. Coding scripts to make engaging transformations require experimenting, experimenting, and more experimenting. I really enjoyed this process, and I created transformations including changing textures of the buildings and playing a video as a building’s texture. Layers of transformations are exciting and serve as beneficial feedback to the user’s presence in the environment.
